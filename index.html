🧑‍🚀Who Am I?

It all started with a curious click. One day, I was casually browsing the internet when I stumbled upon the Inspect Element option — and suddenly, a whole new world opened up in front of me. Lines of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript whispered the secrets of how the web worked, and I knew I had to learn more.

Fast forward to today — I’m Alok, a 20-year-old web developer currently studying Data Science and Applications at IIT Madras. I’ve spent countless hours exploring how to turn ideas into functional, beautiful digital experiences.

Whether it’s a personal portfolio, or a real-world hackathon project that helps people stay safer on the roads like Rakshamarg, I bring the same excitement to every line of code I write. My journey isn't just about learning new frameworks or languages — it’s about solving problems, telling stories through interfaces, and making tech that feels human.

And if you ever find me lost in thought, chances are I’m either debugging something interesting or sipping on a cup of chai, sketching out the next big idea.
